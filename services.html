    • Big Event

    What: The Big Event is a school-wide volunteering event designed to give back to the College Station-Bryan community. Students either signup as an individual or a group and help with yard work, painting, and any other work needed by the residents.

    How to Volunteer: You can go the event website here and register to be a volunteer. You can either register as an individual or a group. Many organizations also sign-up as a group and if there are too many people, they will be assigned to multiple residents and the group will have to split up into smaller groups.

    Austin Pets Alive

    What: Austin Pets Alive is an animal shelter that helps animals get foster and adoptive homes. Volunteers take on jobs such as playing with and walking the animals, cleaning, and performing minor medical care if needed.

    How to Volunteer: Go to their website here and fill out the volunteering form. Anyone under the age of 18 will need a guardian to sign the form and anyone under the age of 16 will need a guardian to be present at the volunteering site.

    Austin Marathon

    What: Volunteers at Austin Marathon will help run the event smoothly by handing out water and snacks to runners, guiding runners and guests to the correct direction, and help passing out the finish medals. Volunteers with cars might also get the task of picking up materials and dropping them back off at the end of the event.

    How to Volunteer: Find the register button on their website here and pick the desired position and the date. Normally the marathon lasts two to three days so volunteers can go all days or just one day.
