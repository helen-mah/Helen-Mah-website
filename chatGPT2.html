Parks in College Station, TX

Lick Creek Park

Lick Creek Park is a peaceful oasis with over 300 acres of natural beauty and recreational opportunities. Hike or bike the trails, fish in the creek, picnic with family and friends, or just relax and enjoy the scenery.

Central Park

Central Park is a family-friendly park located in the heart of College Station. With its lush green spaces, playgrounds, and picnic areas, it's the perfect place to spend a sunny afternoon.

Lyle Dunnam Park

Lyle Dunnam Park offers a variety of recreational opportunities, including a pool, sports fields, playgrounds, and a picnic area. It's a great place for families and sports enthusiasts alike.
